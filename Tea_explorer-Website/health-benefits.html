General Health Benefits of Tea

Tea is not only a delicious beverage, but it also offers numerous health benefits. Some of the general health benefits of tea include:

  • Rich in antioxidants that help protect the body against damage from harmful free radicals
  • Promotes heart health by reducing the risk of heart disease and stroke
  • Boosts immune system function
  • May help prevent certain types of cancer
  • May improve mental alertness and cognitive function
  • May help reduce stress and promote relaxation
